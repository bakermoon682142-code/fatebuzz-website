Support for FateBuzz

Welcome to the Support page for FateBuzz. We are committed to providing you with the best experience possible. Since our app does not use a user account system, most issues can be resolved locally on your device.

1. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Does the app collect my personal data? A: No. We do not require registration or personal information (like your name or email) to use the app. We only collect anonymous usage data via Firebase to improve the app's performance.

Q: Why do I see advertisements? A: Ads allow us to provide and maintain the app for free. You may see personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your App Tracking Transparency (ATT) settings.

Q: How do I reset the app data? A: Since we do not have a user system, all your settings are stored locally. To reset, you can simply delete and reinstall the app. Please note that this will permanently erase any local progress or preferences.

Q: The app is crashing or not loading. What should I do? A: Please ensure you are running the latest version of iOS and the latest version of FateBuzz. Try closing the app from the multitasking view and restarting it.
